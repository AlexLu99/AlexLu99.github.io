I recently completed my M.S. at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (admitted by recommendation), where I research in the CVPR Lab on image stylization and generation. My work designs generative models that produce faithful, controllable stylized imagery for portraits, photos, and video. I am applying for a PhD position in computer vision and generative modeling.

Research Interests

Image Generation · Facial & Photo Stylization · Generative Models (GANs) · Computer Vision

I am interested in how to make stylized image and video generation both expressive and faithful — through semantic preservation, pseudo-paired supervision, style-adaptive feature modules, and temporal consistency for video. More broadly, I want to build controllable generative models that bridge realism and artistic style.
